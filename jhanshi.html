JHANSI TRIP

4 Days/3 Nights



Package Overview


The legendary city of Jhansi has gained popularity especially among the history buffs. Tourists visiting Jhansi can relish the artistic grandeur of the Chandelas which is clearly evident in the imposing forts and palaces. These magnificent structures narrate tales about the courage and struggles of the bygone era.

A walk through this city in Uttar Pradesh takes the travellers to the tourist attractions which are historically significant. The main draw includes the Jhansi Fort which was the venue of the battle between Rani Lakshmi Bai and the British forces. Heading to Parichha, tourists can get a view of a scenic dam, temple, mosque and a Buddhist monastery. Further, a trip to Rani Mahal which was once the palace of Rani Lakshmi and has now been converted into Rani Jhansi Museum will give an insight into the glorious past of the region. A visit to see the Barua Sagar Lake is ideal which is situated at the banks of the Betwa River offering excellent view of the surrounding landscape.

Other than visiting these tourist attractions, one can also make a visit to the Maharaja Gangadhar Rao ki Chhatri, Ganesh Mandir and Mahalakshmi Temple. Jhansi has a lot to offer to those with the love for shopping which is an experience in itself. Tourists can shop for the most precious curios and antiques. What fascinates the most about the marketplaces of Jhansi is the colorful atmosphere which creates a medieval aura. Tourists can take back home some of the royal paintings and artifacts as souvenirs. When it comes to buying some of the intricate handicrafts, the glass bangles would be an ideal choice which one can find in a variety of colours and designs. In addition to this, if tourists happen to visit Jhansi during February and March, they will get an opportunity to witness Jhansi Mahotsav which showcases the rich culture and tradition of Bundelkhand.
