SAGAR TRIP

4 Days/3 Nights



Package Overview

Sagar is a city, municipal corporation and administrative headquarter in Sagar district of the state of Madhya Pradesh in central India. Situated on a spur of the Vindhya Range, 1,758 feet (536 m) above sea-level. The city is around 172 kilometres (107 mi) northeast of state capital, Bhopal. Sagar has been selected as one of the hundred Indian cities to be developed as a smart city under Prime Minister Narendra Modi's flagship Smart Cities Mission and listed top safest city of India 2018.


Day wise Itinerary


DAY 1 : Gadpehra

GADPEHRA

No Entry Fee

It is also called old Sagar,was the capital of the Dangi Kingdom. It is situated six miles north of Sagar Jhansi road.



DAY 2 : Rangir

Rangir

No Entry Fees

A village situated 10 miles from Rehli and 21 miles from Sagar on Sagar Rehli Road on the bank of the Dahar river.


DAY 3 : Rahatgarh Water falls and Fort

RAHATGARH WATER FALLS

No Entry Fee

A small town 60 km west of Sagar on Bhopal Sagar Road renowned for its battlement ramparts, its gates and its ruins of palaces temples and mosques.

RAHATGARH FORT

No Entry Fee

Rahatgarh Fort is situated on the top of north west peak along the range of hills approached by means of a long winding passage.

DAY 4: Aapchand and atal park

AAPCHAND

No Entry Fee

In the ravines of river Gadheri,ensconced in the dense growth of Abchand reserve forest, about 22 miles east of Sagar on the Sagar-Damoh road.

ATAL PARK

No Entry Fee
